RAILWAY DEPARTMENTS
A typical division has an average track length of about 1,000 km and staff strength of about 15,000. All the departments and services of the Indian Railways are represented in a Division.
  1. 0
    Engineering Department
    Maintenance of all fixed assets of the Division, i.e. Track, Bridges, Buildings, Roads, Water supply etc.

    Show feedback
  2. 1
    Mechanical Engineering & Power Department
    Maintenance of all rolling stock of the Division (except electric locos and EMU/MEMUs), i.e. locomotives, passenger and freight cars; and technical super etc.

    Show feedback
  3. 2
    Electrical Engineering Department
    Maintenance of all electric locomotives, EMUs/MEMUs and fixed electrical assets of the Division.

    Show feedback
  4. 3
    Signal & Telecommunication Engineering Department
    Management of the Signalling and Telecommunication (S&T) infrastructure of the division for Safe Train operations.
